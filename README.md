I build AI systems that go past the prototype stage. My work sits at the intersection of research and applied engineering, and I care about the gap between the two.

Right now I am working on genomic foundation models for antimicrobial resistance prediction at bioMérieux, in collaboration with Mila (Chandar Lab).

I also founded and run [HER Innovation Lab](https://her-innovationlab.com/), a women-led AI engineering collective, fully remote, where I manage a team of engineers and we deliver custom AI solutions to clients across industries. We work on LLM agents, RAG systems, data pipelines and automation tools, from scoping to deployment. It started as a side project and turned into something I am genuinely proud of.

Before that, I did a research internship at LIRIS lab on algorithmic reasoning in LLMs, which led to a paper published at RJCIA 2025 and on arXiv (2026).
